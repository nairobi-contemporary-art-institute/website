# Proposed Sitemap Strategy (V1 Enhanced)

Based on the Whitney Museum benchmark, this sitemap embellishes the original NCAI Content Tree to maximize value, SEO, and user engagement.

## 1. Information & Visit
*   **Home** (`/`)
    *   **Timeline Teaser**: A visual hook showcasing 3-4 pivotal moments in EA art history.
*   **About Us** (`/about`)
    *   Mission & Vision
    *   **Institutional Timeline**: Mapping NCAI's legacy alongside broader regional milestones.
    *   History
    *   Team
    *   **Careers & Opportunities** (Elevated from Support)
*   **Visit** (`/visit`)
    *   Location & Hours
    *   **Accessibility** (New: Dedicated page for inclusive access info)
    *   **Gallery Policies** (New: Dedicated page for clear rules)
    *   Map & Directions
*   **Contact** (`/contact`)

## 2. Programmes (The Core)
*   **Exhibitions** (`/exhibitions`)
    *   Current
    *   Upcoming
    *   Past (Archive)
    *   *Individual Exhibition Pages* (Rich media, images, curator text)
*   **Artists** (`/artists`)
    *   Index A-Z
    *   *Artist Profiles* (Bio, Works, Linked Exhibitions)
*   **Events** (`/events`)
    *   Calendar
    *   Registration
*   **Education** (`/education`) (Refined Segmentation)
    *   **Timeline: Immersive EA Art History** (`/timeline` or `/education/art-history`): The flagship interactive experience.
    *   **UJUZI** (Mentorship & Professional Dev)
    *   **Schools & Educators** (Resources, Visits)
    *   **Families & Communities** (Workshops)
*   **Library** (`/library`)

## 3. The Channel & Research (Expanded)
*   **The Channel** (`/channel`) - *The Digital Wing*
    *   **Watch** (Video content, Artist Films)
    *   **Listen** (Podcasts, Audio Guides)
    *   **Read** (**Essays**, Interviews, Reviews) - *Renamed from "Reviews"*
    *   **Digital Commissions** (New: Space for digital-first art, aka "Artport")
*   **Collection** (`/collection`)
    *   Browser/Search
    *   *Archive Item Pages*

## 4. Support & Commerce
*   **Support Us** (`/support`)
    *   Membership
    *   Donate
    *   Partnerships
*   **Shop** (`/shop`) (V2 Foundation)
    *   Publications
    *   Merchandise

## URL Structure & i18n
All routes available with locale prefixes as defined in requirements.
*   `/en/visit`
*   `/sw/visit`
*   `/ar/visit`
*   etc.
